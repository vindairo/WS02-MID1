About Finn the Human

Finn Mertens (mainly known as Finn the Human) is one of the main protagonists of Adventure Time. A fun loving, energetic hero that lives in a magic world called The Land of Ooo with his best friend, Jake the Dog.

Although Finn may not be the most powerful character in the series, or rather in fiction, he is however one of the most iconic cartoon characters ever made, that is until it's revealed that he's more powerful that what people initially imagined.
